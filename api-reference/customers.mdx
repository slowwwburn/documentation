title: "Overview"
---

A Business Customer is an individual that transacts under a Business. They have their own wallets, which allow them to receive funds, make transfers, and perform other financial activities.

## What Can a Business Customer Do?

Wallet Access: Each customer has a wallet for storing and transferring funds.

Transactions: Customers can send and receive money within the Business network or to external accounts.

Transaction Tracking: View wallet balance, transaction history, and details.

Security Features: Transactions require authentication (OTP).

## Prerequisites

The Business must be verified before adding Customers.

Once verified, each Customer receives a unique identifier and wallet access.

Security feature (OTP) must be enabled for safe transactions.
