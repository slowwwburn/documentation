title: "Overview"
---

A Business operates under an Organization and has its own wallet to manage transactions. It can send and receive funds, process payments for it's customers, and interact with the financial system.

## What Can a Business Do?

onboard customers: Businesses can onboard cuustomers only after KYB verification.

Wallet Management: Businesses have dedicated wallets to hold and move funds.

Fund Transfers: Ability to send money within the ecosystem or to external bank accounts.

## Prerequisites:

The Business must provide necessary documentation for verification before onboarding a Customer.

Once verified, the Business receives a unique identifier and wallet access.
